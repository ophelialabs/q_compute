Overview

Geofencing uses virtual boundaries to detect when a device or asset enters or exits a defined area. This supports items such as fleet tracking, asset monitoring, access control, and location-based alerts.

Enterprise geofencing

  • Asset tracking and inventory visibility
  • Fleet management and route optimization
  • Workforce presence verification
  • Security and access control

Space fence technology

Space Fence systems monitor objects in Earth orbit and near-Earth space using radar and tracking infrastructure. They help identify satellites, debris, and other objects for operational awareness.

Key takeaways

  • Geofencing combines location data with rule-based event detection.
  • Space surveillance extends these ideas to orbital tracking and conjunction monitoring.
  • Both systems depend on accurate positioning, timely updates, and clear alerting logic.
