# ESA (European Space Agency Quantum Control)

This directory contains resources and documentation for quantum control systems managed or researched by the European Space Agency (ESA).

## Overview
ESA is a leader in quantum satellite research, focusing on secure quantum communication, quantum key distribution, and advanced satellite management. This directory may include:
- Protocols for quantum communication
- ESA quantum satellite mission documentation
- Research papers and technical specifications

## Applications
- Secure satellite-to-ground and inter-satellite quantum links
- Quantum cryptography for space assets
- Collaboration with international quantum networks
