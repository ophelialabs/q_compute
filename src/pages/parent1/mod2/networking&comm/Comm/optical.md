# Optical communications

This page has been simplified to plain markdown so it can render correctly in the site build.

## Key idea

Optical communication uses light to transmit information over fiber or free-space links.

## Main components

- Source: laser or LED
- Channel: optical fiber or free-space path
- Detector: photodiode or coherent receiver

## Why it matters

Optical links offer high bandwidth, low loss, and strong immunity to electromagnetic interference.
